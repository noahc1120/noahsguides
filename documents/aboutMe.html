About Me

Hi! I'm Noah Chandler, a student currently learning computer programming!
This website is a little project of mine and is intended to help other students in my high-school with academic studies! I am currently hosting a few math study guides, but feel free to contribute to the sourecode on the GitHub!

Website & the Future

Currently noahsguides will mainly feature academic-guided content, ranging from study guides to student tips. In the coming months however a major overhaul will revamp the site into more of a wiki-based site. A forum and quizzing system is planned to be implemented as well.
